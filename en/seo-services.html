Process

What serious SEO work looks like.

1

Audit and keyword research

Technical audit, competitor analysis, selection of keywords that have a realistic chance of bringing business.

2

On-page optimization

Title tags, meta descriptions, heading hierarchy, internal linking, schema markup, image optimization.

3

Technical SEO

Site speed, Core Web Vitals, sitemap, robots.txt, canonical tags, mobile-first indexing.

4

Content and link building

Blog strategy, articles targeting specific queries, quality backlinks from relevant sources.

5

Monthly reports

Search Console, Analytics, position tracking — clear visibility into what was done and what worked.

6

Iteration

SEO is continuous. Every month we adjust strategy based on data, not assumptions.

What to expect

SEO isn't magic. But it is measurable.

First results typically visible within 3–4 months. Significant gains — within 6–9 months. It's essential we agree from the start on what we measure and why.

  • Ranking for keywords in your title tag
  • Organic traffic growth from Google
  • Conversions from organic search (calls, forms, purchases)
  • Core Web Vitals in "Good" range
