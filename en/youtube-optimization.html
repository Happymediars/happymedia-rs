FAQ

Honest questions. Honest answers.

How quickly will I see results?

Optimization of existing videos shows results in 2–4 weeks (impressions and CTR boost). Channel growth (subscribers, watch time) typically takes 3–6 months of consistent work. The YouTube algorithm rewards consistency — don't drop 10 videos and stop.

Do I have to film new videos?

Not necessarily. Clients often have 50+ underoptimized videos — we bring them back to life with new titles, thumbnails and descriptions. In parallel we build a content plan for new videos so the channel keeps moving forward. You can see growth without filming anything new.

What if I don't have enough viewers to monetize?

YouTube monetization (1000 subscribers + 4000 watch hours) comes naturally once strategy works. But for businesses that's often not the goal — the goal is for the channel to bring clients, not AdSense pennies. One video with 5000 views can bring more clients than full monetization ever will.

Do you also optimize YouTube Shorts?

Yes. Shorts run on a separate algorithm with different logic — focus on the hook in the first 3 seconds, pacing, retention. In the Channel Manager package we run Shorts strategy in parallel with long-form videos.

Can you help with filming and editing?

Not directly — we focus on strategy, optimization and growth. But we have vetted partners for filming (Belgrade) and video editors (remote) we recommend. We often pair clients with the right people.

How does it fit with SEO and Google Ads?

YouTube is the second-largest search engine and Google often ranks YouTube videos in top positions. An optimized video can pull traffic from Google, YouTube and Google Ads (video campaigns). Best results come when SEO, Ads and YouTube run as one system.
